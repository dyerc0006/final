Prevention is the key to keeping your furry friend healthy and happy!

Fun Facts!

  • Most cats are actually lactose intolerant and shouldn't be given any dairy products!

  • Dental care for dogs is important, but human toothpaste is toxic to dogs! Make sure you use a pet-safe toothpaste for cleaning your friend's pearly whites.

Why Is Preventative Care Important?

If a problem can be found and addressed early, it's more likely to resolve with:

  • Less expense
  • Less difficulty
  • A higher rate of success

Your veterinarian can recommend wellness care for your pet based on their:

  • Breed
  • Age
  • Overall Health
